About.

AutoFrameCAD© is a powerful BricsCAD add-on/plug-in designed to automate the process of creating frameworks. Whether you're working on architectural, structural, or industrial projects, AutoFrameCAD© simplifies and accelerates the framework design process. With its user-friendly interface and advanced features, AutoFrameCAD© empowers engineers and designers to efficiently generate precise and customizable frameworks with minimal effort.

Key Features

  • Automated framework creation
  • Customizable and precise design
  • User-friendly interface
  • Supports architectural, structural, and industrial projects
  • Time-saving and efficient
